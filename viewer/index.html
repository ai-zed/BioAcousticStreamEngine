BioAcoustic Stream Engine (BASE) · Blenheim Palace Innovation Team
How BASE Works
🎙
Live listening
Microphones stream audio continuously from the estate, day and night.
📊
Seeing sound
Each sound is turned into a spectrogram — a visual fingerprint showing how frequencies change over time.
🤖
Species identification
Machine learning models compare fingerprints against thousands of known recordings and report a species match with a confidence percentage.
🌱
Soil health
Underground sensors pick up acoustic activity in the soil. Healthy soil is full of microbial life — BASE measures this as an indicator of soil biodiversity.
